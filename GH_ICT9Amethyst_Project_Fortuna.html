Fires can happen anywhere, at any time. Knowing how to prevent it is key.
It's easy to overlook popular causes of fire, so here are some tips to remember before it's too late.

TIPS TO PREVENT FIRE

Unplugging appliances you aren't actively using drastically lower chances of fire and even save electricity.
Don't keep your kitchen appliances unattended to if you don't know what you're doing.
Plug important appliances such as appliances near the shower, laundry room or any area near water into a GFCI, or a ground fault circuit interrupter, these prevent said electric appliances from shocking you.
